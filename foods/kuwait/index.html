Famous foods top 5

  1. Machboos: Machboos is a traditional dish made of rice, meat (usually chicken or lamb), and a blend of spices including saffron, cinnamon, and cardamom. It is often served with a side of yogurt or salad.
  2. Mandi: Mandi is a dish that originated in Yemen but is popular in Kuwait. It consists of slow-cooked meat (usually lamb or chicken) and rice, flavored with a variety of spices including cumin and coriander.
  3. Thareed: Thareed is a traditional Arabian dish made with bread, meat (usually lamb or chicken), and a tomato-based sauce. It is often served during Ramadan and other special occasions.
  4. Harees: Harees is a porridge-like dish made with wheat, meat (usually lamb), and spices. It is a popular dish during Ramadan and other special occasions.
  5. Shakshouka: Shakshouka is a dish made with eggs cooked in a tomato-based sauce with onions, peppers, and spices. It is popular in many Middle Eastern countries, including Kuwait.

Famous Sweets

  1. Gers ogaily: Gers ogaily is a sweet pastry made with dates, flour, and butter. It is a popular dessert in Kuwait and can be found in many bakeries and coffee shops.
  2. Luqaimat: Luqaimat is a sweet fried dumpling made with flour, yeast, and sugar. It is often served with a syrup made with honey and saffron.
  3. Balaleet: Balaleet is a sweet vermicelli pudding made with sugar, rose water, and saffron. It is often served for breakfast or as a dessert.
