How to Get Around by Public Transportation

Bus Routes in Jeddah

Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, has public transport buses, which run from 5:30 am to 11:30 pm. These buses are operated by the Saudi Public Transport Company (SAPTCO), and serve as the main public transport system for residents of Jeddah and other cities, such as Mecca, Riyadh, Dammam, and Medina.

There are 6 main bus routes in Jeddah. The Route 7 bus runs from Bin Laden Mosque and Saudi Airlines; while Route 8 covers Bin Laden Mosque, Vegetable Circle, and Security Force Hospital. Moreover, Route 9 bus covers Bin Laden Mosque and Periodic Inspection.
