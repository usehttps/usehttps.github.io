Naples

How to Get Around by Public Transportation

  1. Collier Area Transit (CAT): This is the primary public transportation system serving the Naples area. CAT operates buses on several routes throughout Naples, as well as in some of the neighboring communities. These buses run on a regular schedule and provide service to many of the major shopping centers, hospitals, and other destinations in the area.
    https://www.ridecat.com/
  2. Trolley Service: Naples offers a free trolley service that runs from the Naples Pier to the north end of the city. The trolley runs every day of the week and offers a convenient way to get around the downtown area.
  3. Naples Airport Shuttle: The Naples Airport Shuttle offers transportation between Naples and the Southwest Florida International Airport, which is located in Fort Myers, about 30 miles north of Naples.
  4. Ride Share Services: Popular ride-sharing services such as Uber and Lyft are also available in Naples.
