Singapore

🌆 MUST VISIT IN SINGAPORE

🏙️ City Highlights

  • Marina Bay Sands SkyPark – breathtaking skyline views

  • Gardens by the Bay – Supertree Grove, Cloud Forest & Flower Dome

  • Merlion Park – the iconic photo spot

  • ArtScience Museum – futuristic interactive exhibits

  • Singapore Flyer – observation wheel with panoramic views

  • Clarke Quay – riverside nightlife & dining

🌿 Nature & Culture

  • Singapore Botanic Gardens – UNESCO World Heritage Site

  • Sentosa Island – beaches, Universal Studios, SEA Aquarium, SkyHelix

  • Chinatown – temples, souvenirs, and hawker eats

  • Little India – color, culture, and spices

  • Kampong Glam & Haji Lane – boutiques, cafés, and street art

🎨 Hidden Gems

  • Jewel Changi Airport – Rain Vortex waterfall & Canopy Park

  • Southern Ridges & Henderson Waves – scenic walk connecting parks

  • Tiong Bahru – heritage neighborhood with indie cafés

  • Gillman Barracks – art galleries + brunch spots

  • Pulau Ubin – rustic island village by ferry

🚇 PUBLIC TRANSPORT GUIDE TO SINGAPORE

🚆 MRT (Mass Rapid Transit)

  • Clean, punctual, and covers all major attractions.

  • Operates: 6 AM – 12 midnight

  • Payment:

    • EZ-Link card (top-up card)

    • or contactless Visa/Mastercard/Apple Pay/Google Pay (tap directly at gate)

  • Best app: MyTransport.SG or Citymapper Singapore

🚌 Buses

  • Complement MRT routes, especially for short distances.

  • Same card payment system as MRT.

  • Real-time bus info via Google Maps or SG BusLeh app.

🚖 Taxis & Ride-Hailing

  • Use Grab, Gojek, or ComfortDelGro apps.

  • Safe, metered, and convenient for families or late nights.

🧭 HOW TO USE PUBLIC TRANSPORT IN SINGAPORE

  1. Get a payment method:

    • Buy an EZ-Link card (MRT station ticket offices, 10 SGD)

    • OR use your contactless bank card directly.

  2. Tap in and out:

    • Tap at entry and exit gates (MRT) or card readers (buses).

  3. Transfer between lines:

    • Follow color-coded line signs — transfers are clearly marked.

  4. Plan routes easily:

    • Use Google Maps or MyTransport.SG (live train/bus times).

  5. Avoid fines:

    • Eating/drinking/smoking is prohibited on public transport (fine up to SGD 500).

💡 TIPS & TRICKS FOR TRANSPORTATION IN SINGAPORE

  • 🚉 Singapore Tourist Pass: unlimited MRT/bus rides for 1–3 days (SGD 20–30).

  • 🕓 Avoid rush hours (7–9 AM, 6–8 PM).

  • 🌧 MRT stations are connected to malls—great for rainy days.

  • 💸 Taxi surcharges apply late nights & airport pickups.

  • 🚶 Many attractions are within walking distance from MRT exits.

  • 🧒 Strollers & wheelchairs are welcome on MRT and buses.

  • 🔋 Bring a power bank — ticket machines and Wi-Fi kiosks need mobile data for route checks.

🗓️ 3-NIGHT ITINERARY PLAN

Day 1 – Marina Bay & Gardens

  • Arrive, check in (Marina Bay / Clarke Quay area)

  • Visit Gardens by the Bay, Cloud Forest, SkyPark

  • Evening: Spectra Light Show + dinner at Satay by the Bay

Day 2 – Culture & Heritage

  • Morning: Chinatown + Maxwell Food Centre lunch

  • Afternoon: Little India, Kampong Glam, Haji Lane

  • Evening: Clarke Quay river cruise + night market browsing

Day 3 – Sentosa Fun

  • Full day: Sentosa (Universal Studios / SEA Aquarium / Siloso Beach)

  • Evening: return for shopping at Orchard Road or Jewel Changi

🗓️ 5-NIGHT ITINERARY PLAN

Days 1–3: Same as 3-night plan

Day 4 – Nature & Wildlife

  • Singapore Botanic Gardens (Orchid Garden)

  • Tiong Bahru cafés

  • Night Safari / River Wonders in evening

Day 5 – Relax & Explore

  • Southern Ridges or Mount Faber morning hike

  • Last-minute shopping at Bugis Street / VivoCity

  • Dinner at Lau Pa Sat or Makansutra Gluttons Bay

🗓️ 10-NIGHT ITINERARY PLAN

Days 1–5: Follow above itinerary

Day 6 – Jurong Area

  • Science Centre Singapore, Jurong Bird Park, Japanese Gardens

Day 7 – Pulau Ubin Adventure

  • Ferry from Changi Point Ferry Terminal

  • Cycling, Chek Jawa Wetlands, seafood lunch

Day 8 – Museums & Art

  • National Museum, National Gallery, Asian Civilisations Museum

Day 9 – Local Vibes

  • Gillman Barracks, Tiong Bahru, Henderson Waves

Day 10 – Leisure & Departure

  • Spa or pool morning

  • Jewel Changi Canopy Park & Waterfall before flight

💰 BUDGET TRAVELER TO SINGAPORE

  • Hostels or budget hotels (Bugis, Chinatown, Little India)

  • SGD 25–60 per night

Meals:

  • Hawker centres: SGD 4–7 per meal

Transport:

  • Singapore Tourist Pass for unlimited MRT/bus rides

Free / Cheap Attractions:

  • Gardens by the Bay (outdoor), Botanic Gardens, Henderson Waves, Marina Bay light show, Jewel Changi waterfall

Daily Budget Estimate:
SGD 70–100 (~USD 50–75)
