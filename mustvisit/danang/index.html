Danang

Where to go in Da Nang:

1. Beach : the first place you should enjoy.

2. Marble Mountains (Nui ngu hanh son): quite nature mountain, not very high and not very man-made compared with Bana Hills which was recently built.

3. Ba Na Hills: built recently as a resort. Worth a try by I prefer Marble mountains before going here. There, you can take the picture of Golden Hands at the Golden Bridge.

4. Dragon brigde: The Dragon Bridge in Da Nang hosts a spectacular fire and water show every Friday, Saturday, and Sunday night at 9:00 PM. During the performance, the dragon breathes fire and sprays water, creating a mesmerizing display that lasts approximately 15 minutes.

It’s also very good to take a picture at day time.

What to eat in Da Nang:
