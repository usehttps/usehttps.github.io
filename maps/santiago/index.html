Foods you must try

Famous foods top 5

  1. EMPANADAS, delicious dough pastries (baked or fried) filled with tasty cheese, succulent seafood, beef, olive oil, and onion.
  2. HUMITAS, the Chilean version of tamales. Sweet or savory, Humitas are made with boiled flour and corn meal. Corn husks are stuffed with ground corn, basil, onion, and ground paprika. They are then tied together with thread or twine. Humitas are cooked to perfection by either boiling or steaming in hot water.
  3. COMPLETOS, Chilean version of Hot Dogs.
  4. CEVICHE, is made with raw fish like halibut marinated in citric juices and is usually served with cilantro, cumin, onions, sautéed garlic, and olive oil. If you want to try something different, you could also go for the cold shrimp Ceviche.
  5. ASADO, barbecues
