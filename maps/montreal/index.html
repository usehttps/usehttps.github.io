Food trivia

  • Delicious! C'est bon! means “It’s delicious”
  • Icewine is very famous in Canada. To produce Icewine, grapes are left on the vine until a sustained temperature of -8°C or below is reached, at which time they are hand picked. From there, they must be pressed before they thaw. The result is a juice that is high in sugar and acid, and concentrated with rich, delicious flavours.
