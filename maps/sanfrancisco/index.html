Tips

  1. Clipper Card: Clipper is the all-in-one transit card used for contactless fare payments throughout the San Francisco Bay Area. https://www.clippercard.com/ClipperWeb/
    Clipper customers get discounts on one-way fares of 50 cents for adults, 25 cents for youth and 19 cents for senior and disabled riders.
  2. Apps
    1. Offline guide map: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.mytripnavi.app.SANFRANCISCO
